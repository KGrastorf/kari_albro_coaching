About Me

The Lusciousness of Kari Albro’s Life
A mother of an amazing son, Christopher, born October ’83. Daughter Amber lived only until she was 3 years old. She is close to her heart, born May ’87.

She has an amazing partner, Kevin who has changed her life in so many beautiful ways. True, deep, luscious love can be found at an older age. At 51 she never thought possible, but let her tell you it can be!!

She’s lived in Montana. Growing up on a ranch. She has lived in Mexico, Central America, Fiji, Hawaii, North Carolina and Wisconsin. Montana is her home. She truly loves to travel and especially live out of the country for periods of time.

She has a deep passion for alternative health. You can say she geeks out on this sort of thing. She is a Massage Therapist doing Hospice Massage and Healing Massage. Shelves to communicate with her hands. She has done Home Care, owned a Health Center before becoming a professional Coach.
She has studied, trained and is Certified with Health Coach Institute, went on to HMBA: Mastering the Business and Art of Coaching. She is a certified Fermentationist with FCP Summer Bock. Taken Marie Folio’s Business School. Andrea Nakayama’s Digestive Intensive Program. She is currently taking training with Layla Martin in Sex, Love and Relationship Coaching.
